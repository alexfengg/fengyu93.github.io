### Descriptions
Class Hours: 4  
Lab and Field Work Hours: 0

Prerequisite(s): Grade of “C” (2.0) or better in MAC 2312, or score of 5 on the Calculus BC Advanced Placement Exam, or C.I. Corequisite(s): None. Prerequisite(s) or orequisite(s): None.
