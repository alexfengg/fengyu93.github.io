Welcome

I am a postdoc is a Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University at Albany, SUNY, under the supervision of Prof. Yiming Ying at ML@UA Lab.. I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in mathematics at University of Central Florida after I got B.S. in mathematics and B.E. in economics at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, China. My current research interests are mainly in the areas of Statistical Learning Theory, Machine Learning, Optimization, and Mathematics of Data Science.
