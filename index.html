Feng Yu

Department of Mathematics University of Central Florida

Biography

Feng Yu is a Ph.D candidate in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Central Florida. He received his M.S. degree in mathematics in 2018 from the University of Central Florida after he got B.S. in mathematics and B.E. in economics from Southwestern University of Finance and Economics, China, in 2016. His research focuses on PDE, optimization. He is currently on the areas of Cyber Physical System and optimization. He received the ORC fellowship from the University of Central Florida in 2016.
